Our Story

Welcome to Cafe Deja Vu — your favourite hangout spot in the heart of Tinsukia, Assam. We started with one simple dream: to create a place where everyone feels at home, the food is always delicious, and the vibes are always good.

Tucked away on Gelapukhuri Road, we serve a wide variety of fast food — from cheesy pizzas and crispy momos to steaming noodles and refreshing cold coffee. Whether you're meeting friends, taking a break from studies, or just craving something tasty, Deja Vu is always the answer.

Every dish is made fresh with care, using quality ingredients. Our team works hard to make sure every visit feels special — because you deserve more than just a meal. You deserve an experience.

"Great food. Great vibes. Every time." — That's not just our tagline. It's our promise. 🤝
